FANUC, the largest maker of industrial robots in the world, organizes each year a competition for robotics enthusiasts. I participated in this competition thanks to my school : Polytech Paris-Saclay. This year the subject lends itself around an assembly line motors (each weighing 5.1 Kg).

We had to conceive an entire work environment where the operator musn't have musculoskeletal disorders or MSD in short (the abreviation is TMS in french).

My team was composed of two other people aside from me and my role, as an IT manager, was to create a simulation of our solution using a software called RoboGuide.
